Before You Book

Scheduling & Policies

Everything you need to know before getting started with one of our programs.

How to Schedule

Contact us by phone to discuss your training needs. Payment must be made before your dog is placed on our training schedule. We accept payment over the phone for your convenience.

Cancellation Policy

We require a 48-hour cancellation notice for all scheduled sessions and board and train programs. Cancellations made within 48 hours may result in charges. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to reschedule.

Board & Train Terms

All board and train programs require a signed contract. A deposit (typically 50% of the total cost) is required to reserve your dog's spot. No refunds on payments, but credits toward future services may be available.
