Required Vaccinations

Essential vaccines for your dog's boarding stay at K9 Solutions Center

Proof of current vaccinations is required for ALL PETS that board at K9 Solutions Center.

  • All vaccines must be administered at least 48 hours prior to check-in
  • All vaccines must be administered by a licensed veterinarian
  • Written proof from your veterinarian is required at check-in

Vaccination Schedule

Bordetella Every 6 months - 1 year depending on vet Kennel cough

Important Note

Due to concerns for human and animal safety, we are unable to make exceptions to vaccination requirements. Please ensure your pet's vaccinations are current and that you bring written proof from your veterinarian at check-in.
