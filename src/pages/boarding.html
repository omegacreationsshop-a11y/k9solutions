Vaccinations

Required for ALL PETS

All vaccines must be administered at least 48 hours prior to check-in

Must be administered by a licensed veterinarian

Written proof of vaccination required at check-in

Important: Due to concerns for human and animal safety, we are unable to make exceptions to vaccination requirements.

View Vaccination Schedule

Medications

Medication administration is provided at no additional charge.

All medications must be provided at check-in

Administration procedures must be documented in writing prior to departure

Bring a complete list of all medications and dosage information

Provide sufficient medication for at least two additional days in case of delayed return

Food Requirements

Please bring your pet's regular food from home. We feed pets twice per day.

Portion Separation

Divide your pet's daily allotment into two separate meals using individual Ziploc baggies.

Canned Food

Label each can with feeding information. If your pet eats less than one can per feeding, please bring a lid to seal the can.

Refrigerated Food

Please inform us if your pet's food requires refrigeration.
