All pets boarding at K9 Solutions must have current vaccinations. Required vaccines include:

  • Rabies (required by state)
  • Distemper
  • Hepatitis
  • Leptospirosis
  • Parainfluenza
  • Parvovirus
  • Bordetella (Kennel Cough)

Important requirements:

  • All vaccines must be administered at least 48 hours prior to check-in
  • All vaccines must be given by a licensed veterinarian
  • Written proof of vaccination is required at check-in

Due to concerns for human and animal safety, we cannot make exceptions to vaccination requirements.

Please bring:

  • Your dog's regular food divided into two separate meals in Ziploc bags (or let us know if you'd prefer us to provide food)
  • Written proof of current vaccinations from your veterinarian
  • Any medications with clear administration instructions
  • Feeding instructions if your dog has special dietary needs
  • Favorite toys (optional, but dogs enjoy them)
  • A blanket or item with your scent (optional)

If you're bringing canned food, please label it clearly and bring a lid if your dog eats less than one can per feeding.
