About K9 Solutions

Dedicated to dog training and pet care excellence

Our Story

K9 Solutions was founded in 2013 with a simple vision: to help dogs and their owners build stronger, happier relationships through professional training and exceptional care. What started as a small operation has grown into one of the most respected dog training facilities in the region.

Our founder, Sarah Mitchell, grew up around dogs and quickly realized that many behavioral issues stem from a lack of proper training and understanding. She became certified as a professional dog trainer and started working with individual clients before establishing K9 Solutions to serve our entire community.

Today, K9 Solutions operates from a state-of-the-art facility featuring training grounds, boarding accommodations, and grooming services. We've had the privilege of working with thousands of dogs and their families, and we're proud of the positive impact we've made in their lives.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional dog training and pet care services that enhance the quality of life for both dogs and their families. We believe every dog deserves the opportunity to be their best self, and every owner deserves professional guidance and support.

We're committed to using only positive reinforcement and science-based training methods that prioritize the well-being and happiness of every dog in our care.

Compassion

We treat every dog with the care and respect they deserve, understanding that they're valued family members.

Excellence

We continuously improve our methods and stay current with the latest in dog behavior science and training techniques.

Partnership

We work closely with owners to ensure training success both at our facility and in their homes.
